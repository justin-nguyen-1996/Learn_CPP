1. Scope vs. Duration
    --> scope refers to where the variable is accessible
    --> duration refers to when the variable is created/destroyed

2. Local Variables
    --> block scope (only visible within the block they are declared)
    --> automatic duration (created when entering the block and destroyed when exiting)
